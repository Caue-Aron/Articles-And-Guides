Visual Elements

Text Blocks

You're looking at one right now!

I like to write this way, separating everything into chunks and naming them so that you know ahead of time what you'll read and decide if it's worth reading or not. Yes, somethings are not meant to be read! I love text resources because they're really fast to look through as a cheat-sheet. In short, writing in this way assures that you'll always know what you'll read. And since you know what you'll read, you can decide if you should read it or not

Blocks are necessary because I'm direct and honest

When I say I'll talk about a topic, you best believe I'll talk about it! You best believe there will be a whole paragraph on that subject and It'll be completelly on you if read it and felt your time wasted at the end! Buddy, you were warned about it!
